What it does

understand your rights.

Nine AI-powered features built on Claude. The legal team you never had.

◈

Risk Scoring

Every ToS gets a score from 1 (safe) to 10 (dangerous) based on clause severity, data-sharing scope, and category weights. Labels: SAFE · CAUTION · RISKY · DANGEROUS.

3 5 8 10
◻

Plain English

Every flagged clause is rewritten in simple language. No law degree required.

◉

Deep Explanation

Click any clause for a deeper breakdown: real-world example, hidden implications, what you can do about it.

◈

Privacy Alternatives

When risk is high (≥7), Claude suggests real privacy-respecting alternatives to the service, cached per domain so they load instantly.

△

Version Diffing

When a site updates their ToS, see exactly what got worse or better since your last visit. Color-coded changes by clause.

◎

Change Alerts

Subscribe to any domain. Get a browser notification the moment their Terms of Service change, even when you're not on the site.

⬡

User Profiles

Switch between General, Journalist, Activist, and Business profiles. The AI adjusts what it flags based on your risk exposure.

▦

PDF Reports

Export a full PDF analysis report with risk score, clause table, and TLDR, ready to save or share.

◳

Analytics Dashboard

Track which sites you've analyzed, average risk scores, most common clause types, and your analysis history.

Chrome extension

Install in 60 seconds.

No Chrome Web Store needed. Load directly from the ZIP file — takes 3 steps.

↓ Download extension ZIP Chrome · Manifest V3 · Free
1

Download & unzip

Click the button above to download tos-reader-extension.zip. Unzip it anywhere on your computer — you'll get a folder called extension/.

2

Open Chrome extensions

In Chrome, go to chrome://extensions (paste into address bar). Enable Developer mode using the toggle in the top-right corner.

💡 Developer mode lets you load extensions from your computer instead of the Chrome Web Store.
3

Load the extension

Click Load unpacked → select the extension/ folder you unzipped. ToS Reader will appear in your extensions list.

That's it. Pin it to your toolbar and visit any Terms of Service page.

How to use it
🌐

Auto-detect

Go to any page with "terms", "privacy", or "legal" in the URL. The extension reads it automatically.

🔍

Click the badge

Click the ToS Reader icon in your toolbar to open the popup with risk score, clauses, and summary.

👤

Pick your profile

Switch between General, Journalist, Activist, or Business — the AI flags different risks for each.

🔔

Subscribe to changes

Click Subscribe in the popup to get notified when the site updates its Terms of Service.

Requires local backend. The extension calls localhost:8000. Download the project and double-click a launcher — no terminal needed:
🍎 Mac double-click start.command 🪟 Windows double-click start.bat ⬇ Download project ZIP includes both launchers
Requires: Docker Desktop (free) · LLMAPI key (free tier available)
